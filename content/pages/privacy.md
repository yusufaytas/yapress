**Last Updated: April 9, 2026**

Your privacy matters to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Analytics

We use privacy-focused analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This includes:

- Page views and navigation patterns
- Referral sources
- Device and browser information
- Geographic location (country level only)

We do not track individual users or collect personally identifiable information through analytics.

### Contact Forms

When you contact us, we collect:

- Your email address
- Your name (if provided)
- Message content

This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties.

## Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies for:

- Session management
- Analytics (anonymized)
- User preferences

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work properly.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

- **Hosting**: Vercel (see their [privacy policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy))
- **Analytics**: Plausible Analytics (privacy-focused, GDPR compliant)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Request access to your data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of analytics tracking
- Update your contact information

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
